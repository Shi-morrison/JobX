{{
  "hard_gaps": ["gap1", "gap2", ...],
  "soft_gaps": ["gap1", "gap2", ...],
  "reframe_suggestions": [
    {{
      "gap": "the missing skill or keyword",

    }}
  ]

- hard_gaps: Skills the candidate genuinely lacks and cannot credibly reframe (e.g. requires 5 years of Kubernetes, candidate has never used it)

- reframe_suggestions: Only for soft_gaps — specific, actionable rewording advice tied to the candidate's actual experience

- Be honest about hard gaps — do not suggest reframing things that are clearly out of reach
- Reframe suggestions must reference the candidate's ACTUAL experience listed below, not hypothetical experience
